# Sponsors

The people and companies who fund Más Bandwidth's open source work directly.
Credit here is with each sponsor's consent, some prefer to stay private, and
that's respected. This page names only those who are glad to be named.

Sponsorship funds *public* open source under github.com/mas-bandwidth (MIT/BSD).
Sponsors help steer what gets prioritized. The work stays open and everyone gets
it. What each month's sponsorship actually funded shows up in the monthly
[ledgers](ledgers/).
